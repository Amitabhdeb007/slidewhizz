FAQ
How does the 48-hour turnaround work?+
Once you submit your brief and content, we start immediately. Most projects are delivered within 48 hours. Rush 24-hour delivery is available on Enterprise plans.
What do you need from me to get started?+
A brief outline of your content, any existing brand guidelines, and your goals for the presentation. We handle the rest — structure, design, and flow.
Do you offer ongoing retainer packages?+
Yes — our retainer clients get discounted per-deck rates, priority scheduling, and a dedicated account manager. Contact us to discuss a custom plan.
What file formats do you deliver?+
We deliver fully editable PowerPoint files by default. PDF, Google Slides, and Keynote formats are available on Professional and Enterprise plans.
